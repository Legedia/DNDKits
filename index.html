D&D Calculators

These calculators are designed to help DMs and players with various aspects of D&D gameplay. Use them to quickly determine character stats, calculate challenge ratings, and generate treasure.

  • Character Stat Calculator: Enter ability scores to calculate modifiers.
  • Challenge Rating (CR) Calculator: Determine the difficulty of an encounter.
  • Treasure Generator: Generate random treasure based on encounter difficulty.

How to Use: Select the calculator you need and input the required values. The results will be displayed instantly.
